Our Story

Two cooks, one grill, and real saffron.

D60 began with a simple idea between two people who moved from Iran to Hamilton: cook the food we grew up on, and the food we fell for here, and refuse to choose between them.

One of us runs the grill. One of us runs the room. The smash patties are pressed to order, the deli is roasted in house, and the saffron in the ice cream and cocktails is the real thing, never a shortcut.

Come for a burger, stay for a faloodeh, and let us feed you the way we feed our own family.
